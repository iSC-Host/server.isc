About it / us


iSC Server is a software system from iSC Group and it was programmed by developers on iSC Dev Contibuting site and GitHub portal.
With iSC Server you can save, manage sync & share your files, website and more on your own Server! Access your data from all your devices, on an open platform you can extend and modify.
